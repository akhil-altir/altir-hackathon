# Altir Tech Day Teaser Design

Date: 2026-05-13
Format: `16:9`
Duration: `45 seconds`
Audio: `music only`
Tone: `cinematic build journey`

## Goal

Create a short teaser video for Altir Tech Day on `May 22, 2026` that builds anticipation around teaming up, building exciting projects, and having fun while shipping something real.

The teaser should feel branded to the existing landing page rather than like a generic hackathon promo.

## Audience

- Event participants
- Internal company audience
- People seeing the teaser on stage, in chat, or in internal social posts

## Core Message

- One day to build
- Two people per team
- Cross-discipline collaboration
- Real tools, real projects, real demos
- Fun and momentum matter as much as pressure

## Creative Direction

The teaser should feel like the existing landing page turned cinematic.

Visual language:
- Near-black background
- Charcoal panel surfaces
- Acid green highlight: `#c4ff00`
- White text and restrained muted greys
- Monospaced typography
- Faint grid and scanline overlays
- Minimal, deliberate use of the Altir logo

Motion language:
- Slow, tense opening
- Gradual ramp into faster kinetic text and panel motion
- Precise reveals and wipes, not noisy glitch spam
- Fastest pace in the build section
- Calm, confident final lockup

## Brand Assets

Use the provided Altir logo variants:
- Black logo mark on light surfaces
- White logo mark on dark surfaces

Logo usage:
- Brief brand hint in the opening
- Strong final lockup at the end
- Avoid overusing the mark mid-video

## Narrative Arc

1. Anticipation
   Introduce the event date and brand with restraint.
2. Invitation
   Establish team formation, building together, and the event premise.
3. Build rush
   Push energy, movement, and pressure upward.
4. Payoff
   Shift from motion to confidence and clarity.
5. Final lockup
   Close on Altir Tech Day and the date.

Structure:
- Opening brand scene
- Stakes scene

- Idea scene
- Build rush scene

Production notes:

- Entrance animations on every scene
